• Frequently Asked Questions •

How long are your contracts?

Mobintouch plans are month-to-month. We make it simple to start or stop your premium account at any time.

Is there a discount for companies?

Yes, with Enterprise plan we offer a 20% discount for companies with a minimum of 10 employees.

How can I manage multiple accounts with the Enterprise plan?

When you take an Enterprise plan, in the settings of your account you can attribute the premium accounts to users inside the mobintouch network.

Can I cancel my premium plan?

Canceling Mobintouch premium plan is an easy, no-questions-asked process. You just need to switch back to the Starter plan.

Can I switch plans?

Yes, at any time on this page. When upgrading or downgrading your account plan, you will receive either a pro-rata charge or refund, depending on the cost of your new plan.

Do you have more questions?

Our support team is available 24/7 and usually responds in well under an hour. Visit our Help Center to contact them.
